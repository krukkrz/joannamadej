OMANTEL

Omantel online store

Omantel is the largest telecommunications company in Oman. In addition to telecommunications services, they also have an online store with electronics.

While working at the Unriddl agency, I had the opportunity to work on the Omantel store project and introduce new features to it.

Below few of the many features that I have introduced:
  • Possibility to pay with points and vouchers
  • Possibility to order internet, prepaid and postpaid in online store
  • Outlet pickup
  • Apple store inside Omantel store (cooperation with another designer)
  • Creating the possibility for external vendors to sell their products in Omantel store in a way that the users will not be aware that they are buying from someone other than Omantel, but that they are aware of essential information, for example, that the order will come in two packages.
