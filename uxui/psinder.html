PSINDER APP

As at work, I am in the project of an online store for a telco brand where I am designing mainly for desktop view, I wanted to practice also my skills in designing a mobile app. This is why I designed the Psinder app.

As I am a fan of dogs (I am owning one by myself) and I am aware that dogs will be able to use telephones, computers, and thereby the Internet in a moment - obviously. I decided not to wait any longer and design mockups for a dog dating app so that every dog will be able to find its love. 💖
