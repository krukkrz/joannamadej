about me

my journey

Hi, I am Asia and I have just started my journey with graphic and UX/UI design. I have been always dreaming to do something connected with design and art but I was always afraid to go for it.

Recently I have dare to quit my job and follow my natural instinct and curiosity. My first thought was to prepare a portfolio so I started designing and improving my skills in adobe software that I already knew. I heard about UX/UI and I went for Aga Naplocha lecture – UX-owa zupa instant, czyli o projektowaniu doświadczeń usera. Inspired by Aga, by the use of here online course I have coded my first website, which you are watching right now. I have coded it in:

  • html
  • CSS
When designing I am using:
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe InDesign
  • Figma

I am learning more and more about graphic and UX/UI design and I am working on expanding my portfolio. I am looking for a job in this areas so If you know anything about any, feel free to contact me!
