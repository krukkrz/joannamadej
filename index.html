ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Asia and I am a Product Designer with 2 years of experience. Before I worked as a Graphic Designer for 2 years. I like to make users' lives easier by improving the products they use.

I have experience in developing wireframes, and prototypes, working with design systems, and conducting usability tests and UX audits. I was also helping Senior Product Designer in performing interviews with users, and workshops with clients.

I'm open to new challenges. If you're looking for someone to work on a UX/UI-related project, feel free to contact me.
